/* How is it deployed? */
    tl;dr: Using GitLab CI for site deployments on Cloudflare Workers, with RTD Community on
           the side.

    I use GitLab CI over at GitLab SaaS (aka https://gitlab.com) thanks to GitLab for Open-source
    Program (https://about.gitlab.com/solutions/open-source/join) with some Nixery (nixery.dev)
    involved on Tangled Spindle (https://blog.tangled.org/ci), previously on Manimum GitLab
    (https://mau.dev) by ~tulir (https://mau.fi), and deployed on both Cloudflare Workers in
    production and RTD Community (https://readthedocs.org).

    You can view the GitLab CI config used for deployments at
    https://gitlab.com/andreijiroh-dev/website/-/blob/main/.gitlab-ci.yml. For Tangled Spindle,
   see https://tangled.org/andreijiroh.dev/site/blob/main/.tangled/workflows/site-deploy.yml.
   Deployments on RTD Community can be tracked separately at https://readthedocs.org/projects/ajhalili2006/.
